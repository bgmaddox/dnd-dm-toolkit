# Factions — Oakhaven Valley

---

## How to Use This File
Each faction entry answers four questions:
1. **What do they want?** — their goal
2. **What are they doing about it?** — their current actions
3. **What do they think of the party?** — disposition and why
4. **Who leads them and who are the key faces?** — link to NPC files

---

## The Circle of Roots

- **Goal:** To protect the Oakhaven Valley from corruption and ensure the health of the Elder Oak.
- **Methods:** Natural magic, animal messengers, and strategic isolationism.
- **Current Status:** Desperate. The blight is spreading, and their traditional rituals are failing to stop it.
- **Disposition toward party:** Suspicious. They see any newcomers as potential agents of the "Iron Axemen" unless proven otherwise.
- **Leader:** High Druid Elara (NPC file pending)
- **Known Members:**
  - [Thistle](npcs/thistle.md)
- **Resources:** Control over the forest's flora and fauna, hidden groves, ancient wisdom.
- **Weakness:** Lack of numerical strength; internal disagreement on how to handle the miners.
- **Notes:** They believe the forest is a living entity that must be appeased.

---

## The Iron Axemen

- **Goal:** To extract the maximum amount of "Iron-Vein" ore and lumber from the valley to fulfill their contracts with the southern kingdoms.
- **Methods:** Industrial-scale mining, heavy fortifications, and hiring mercenaries for protection.
- **Current Status:** Aggressive. They are pushing further into the woods as the easily accessible ore runs dry.
- **Disposition toward party:** Business-like. They view the party as potential "troubleshooters" or guards.
- **Leader:** Captain Horgus (NPC file pending)
- **Known Members:**
  - Sergeant Brak
- **Resources:** Heavy armor, siege equipment, vast financial backing.
- **Weakness:** Reliant on external supply lines; low morale among the common laborers.
- **Notes:** They view the druids as "eccentric hermits" standing in the way of progress.
