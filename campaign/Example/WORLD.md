## Setting Notes

### Geography
The Oakhaven Valley is a secluded basin surrounded by the Mist-Crowned Peaks. The valley floor is dominated by the Great Oakhaven Forest, a dense woodland of towering ancient trees. At its heart lies the Elder Oak, a tree so large it supports a small ecosystem within its branches.

### History
For centuries, the valley has been the sacred domain of the Circle of Roots. However, the recent discovery of "Iron-Vein" ore in the surrounding mountains has brought outsiders. The town of Newforge has sprung up at the valley's edge, serving as a hub for miners and woodcutters.

### Themes
- **Nature vs. Industry:** The tension between the druids' preservation of the forest and the axemen's need for resources.
- **Ancient Secrets:** Ruins of a pre-elven civilization are often found tangled in the roots of the oldest trees.
- **Cycle of Life:** The concept that death in the forest feeds new growth.

---

## Active Situation
The Elder Oak is showing signs of a mysterious blight. The Circle of Roots believes the nearby mining operations are poisoning the groundwater, while the Iron Axemen claim the druids are using magic to sabotage their equipment. Tensions are at a breaking point.

---

## Party Relationships & History
The party has recently arrived in the valley, each drawn by different rumors or duties. They have met at the 'Green Man Inn' in Newforge and are currently deciding whether to take a contract from the miners or investigate the druids' claims.
