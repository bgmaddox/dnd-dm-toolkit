## Personality
**Traits:** I'm always picking up bits of nature — stones, feathers, leaves — and studying them. I feel far more at home among animals than people. I am slow to trust those who smell of smoke and iron.

**Ideals:** Greater Good. It is each person's responsibility to help the land that sustains them. Nature's balance must be maintained at all costs.

**Bonds:** I am the last of my clan, and I will not let their sacred valley fall to the blight. I owe a debt to High Druid Elara for saving me from a blizzard when I was young.

**Flaws:** I am too enamored with the beauty of the forest to see the practical needs of civilization. I find it hard to forgive those who have ever harmed a tree.

## DM Notes
Kaelen serves as a bridge between the party and the Circle of Roots. He is not a member of the Circle himself, but he respects them and shares their goals. He is a man of few words, preferring to let his arrows or his tracking skills do the talking. 

He is currently tracking a group of "blight-touched" wolves that have been harrassing the outskirts of Newforge, and he may ask the party for help if he encounters them.
