# The Elder Oak

## At a Glance
- **Type:** Sentinel Tree; Sacred Grove; Heart of the Valley
- **Region:** The center of the Oakhaven Forest
- **Tone:** Ancient, majestic, but currently tinged with a sense of decaying sickness.
- **Scale:** Massive — the trunk is over 100 feet in diameter, and the lowest branches are 50 feet off the ground.
- **Controlled by:** The Circle of Roots

## Read-Aloud Description
Even before you see it, you feel its presence — a weight in the air, a sense of gravity that belongs to something much larger than a mere plant. Then, the canopy thins, and the Elder Oak reveals itself. It is a titan of wood and leaf, its trunk a wall of furrowed grey bark that seems to support the very sky. Its roots are like frozen rivers, carving deep into the earth and creating valleys and ridges of their own. But as you look closer, the majesty is marred. Some of the leaves are a sickly, bruised purple instead of green, and a faint, sweet-and-sour smell of rot hangs in the air. This is the heart of the valley, and it is beating slowly.

## DM-Only Notes
The Elder Oak is more than a tree; it's a living archive and a conduit for the valley's magic. If it dies, the entire valley will become a wasteland.

The blight is caused by a corruption in the "Deep Roots," where the mining operations of the Iron Axemen have accidentally breached an ancient, sealed chamber containing a primordial shadow. The druids know something is wrong but haven't been able to reach the Deep Roots due to the Axemen's fortifications.

The tree contains:
- **The High Grove:** A platform built into the upper branches where High Druid Elara resides.
- **The Root Tunnels:** Natural passages within the root system, some of which lead to the Deep Roots.
- **The Sap Well:** A natural pool of glowing amber sap that has healing properties (though it is becoming clouded by the blight).

## Key Features
- **The Great Trunk:** Covered in ancient carvings and bioluminescent moss.
- **The Hollow Heart:** A large opening at the base that leads to the inner sanctum of the druids.
- **The Whispering Canopy:** On windy days, the leaves seem to speak in a forgotten tongue.
- **The Blighted Branch:** A massive limb that has completely withered, serving as a warning of what is to come.

## Occupants
- **High Druid Elara:** Usually found meditating in the High Grove.
- **The Root-Guard:** A group of elite druids and awakened shrubs that protect the tree.
- **Thistle:** Often returns here to report on their scouting.

## Connections
- **The Deep Roots:** Accessible via the tunnels; leads to the source of the blight.
- **Newforge:** A two-day trek through the forest; the path is becoming increasingly dangerous.

## Atmosphere & Senses
- **Sound:** The constant, low-frequency hum of the tree's energy; the rustle of leaves that sounds like a million voices.
- **Smell:** Fresh pine and ancient earth, currently clashing with the sharp, chemical scent of the blight.
- **Temperature:** Always exactly 68 degrees Fahrenheit within the tree's shadow, regardless of the weather outside.
