# Ezmerelda d'Avenir

## At a Glance
- **Role:** Vistana monster hunter; potential ally and one of the most capable fighters the party can find
- **Location:** Roving — her wagon can be found near Vallaki or on the Old Svalich Road; she herself may be encountered almost anywhere
- **Stat Block:** Ezmerelda d'Avenir (unique)
- **Faction:** None / Independent

## Appearance
Ezmerelda is a striking Vistana woman in her late twenties with dark curling hair, amber eyes that miss nothing, and a wide mouth that defaults to a sharp, sardonic smile. Her left leg below the knee is a prosthetic of masterwork design — she doesn't hide it and she doesn't explain it unless she trusts you, which takes time.

## Voice & Manner
- **Voice Quirk:** Speaks quickly, pivots between warmth and dry humor and cold competence with no warning. She makes jokes about the things that hurt her most. If she stops joking, pay attention.
- **Physical Tell:** When she's actually angry — not the performed kind — she goes very still, and her right hand settles on the hilt of her blade without gripping it. A lot of people have mistaken that stillness for calm.

## Motivations
- **Want (immediate):** To find and kill whatever supernatural threat is most actively endangering people she's decided to care about. She picks causes fast and commits completely.
- **Want (deep):** To prove — to van Richten, to herself, to the world — that she is not just his student. That she became what she is on her own terms, through her own will, despite what he did.
- **Secret:** She knows where van Richten is hiding. She tracked him down months ago and then... didn't confront him. She told herself she was waiting for the right moment. The truth is she doesn't know what she would say — or what she's more afraid of: that he'll apologize, or that he won't. She is angrier at him than she has admitted to anyone, including herself.

## Relationships
- Dr. Rudolph van Richten: Mentor who trained her, then abandoned her without warning when she was a teenager; she speaks of him with clipped, careful neutrality that fools no one
- Strahd von Zarovich: Primary quarry; she has been hunting him longer than the party has known he exists
- The party (if befriended): She invests fast and hard — she would die for people she respects, and she hates that about herself

## Sample Dialogue
> "Van Richten taught me everything I know about hunting monsters. He also taught me that teachers leave. Useful lessons, both."
> "The leg? Werewolf. I got the better of that conversation eventually."
> "You want my help? Fine. But I lead on anything that involves fire, silver, or the words 'it's probably fine.'"

## DM Notes
Ezmerelda is the party's best potential combat ally and also one of the most emotionally rich NPCs in the module. She works best when the party encounters her before they find van Richten — that way the van Richten reveal lands harder, because they already know her side of the wound.

Her prosthetic leg is a detail to play honestly: it doesn't limit her in combat, but it's visible and it has a story. Let her bring it up on her own terms. If players ask about it immediately, she deflects with humor. If they earn her trust, she tells the truth about the night she lost it — and what van Richten did (or didn't do) after.

Her wagon is trapped (multiple alarms and at least one explosive). If the party tries to break in without her permission, she finds out. She's not vindictive about it — she just wants them to understand that she is not someone you take liberties with.

The van Richten reunion, if the party facilitates it, should be uncomfortable and real. She's not going to cry and forgive him on cue. Play the anger. Let it breathe. Their dynamic is one of the best pieces of character drama the module offers, and it pays off most when it isn't resolved too easily.
