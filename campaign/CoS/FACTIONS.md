# Factions — Curse of Strahd

## Strahd's Court
- **Goal:** Serve Strahd, maintain his control over Barovia, acquire Ireena Kolyana
- **Methods:** Vampire spawn patrol the roads and castle. Werewolf pack terrorizes villages. Hags serve as agents. Ravens are watched as potential spies.
- **Current Status:** Fully active and dominant
- **Disposition toward party:** Hostile — unless Strahd has ordered them to observe rather than attack
- **Leader:** Strahd von Zarovich (see NPC file)
- **Known Members:** Rahadin (chamberlain), Volenta Popofsky, Ludmilla Vilisevic, Anastrasya Karelova (vampire brides), Cyrus Belview (servant)
- **Resources:** Castle Ravenloft, vampire spawn army, werewolf pack, nearly unlimited time
- **Weakness:** Sunlight, running water, the artifacts (Sunsword, Holy Symbol), a stake through the heart while in his coffin
- **Notes:** Strahd is not simply hostile. He is interested in the party. He may invite them to dinner, test them, or even temporarily ally with them against a common enemy. He is never to be trusted.

## The Vistani
- **Goal:** Survival and freedom — maintain their unique status as the only people who can pass through the mists
- **Methods:** Travel between Barovian settlements in their wagons, trade information, tell fortunes, serve as intermediaries
- **Current Status:** Fragmented — most Vistani serve Strahd in exchange for their freedom; a small number oppose him quietly
- **Disposition toward party:** Cautious neutral. Will trade and offer readings. Some will report the party's movements to Strahd.
- **Leader:** Madam Eva (Tser Pool, oldest and most powerful Vistana — secretly the goddess Mother Night's avatar, or something close to it)
- **Known Members:** Arrigal (spy for Strahd), Luvash (camp leader at Vallaki), Arabelle (Luvash's daughter, prophetically significant)
- **Resources:** Freedom of movement through the mists, information network across all of Barovia, Tarokka readings
- **Weakness:** Divided loyalties. Arrigal actively works against the party.
- **Notes:** Madam Eva will give an honest Tarokka reading regardless of her allegiances. The Dark Powers compel her to. She cannot lie about the cards.

## The Keepers of the Feather
- **Goal:** Resist Strahd and protect Barovia's people from within
- **Methods:** Operate in secret as an underground network. Members are wereravens — they appear as ordinary innkeepers, merchants, and travelers. Use ravens to pass messages.
- **Current Status:** Active but cautious. Will not openly confront Strahd's forces.
- **Disposition toward party:** Friendly once trust is established — but they are careful about revealing themselves
- **Leader:** Urwin Martikov (Blue Water Inn, Vallaki) and Davian Martikov (Wizard of Wines winery)
- **Known Members:** Muriel Vinshaw (wandering weraven), most of the Martikov family
- **Resources:** Information, safe houses, the ability to move undetected as ravens, connections to the Wizard of Wines
- **Weakness:** Small numbers, will not risk open combat, rely on secrecy
- **Notes:** Ravens following the party are almost certainly Keepers scouts. A good omen — they track threats to Strahd, not allies of Strahd.

## Church of the Morninglord
- **Goal:** Maintain faith and protect Barovians — though the god feels silent and absent
- **Methods:** The church in the Village of Barovia and St. Andral's Church in Vallaki offer sanctuary and burial rites
- **Current Status:** Weakened. Father Donavich in Barovia village is broken by grief (his son Doru is a vampire spawn in the church basement). Father Lucian in Vallaki is more functional but under pressure from the Baron.
- **Disposition toward party:** Friendly — they represent hope
- **Leader:** No central leadership. Father Donavich (Barovia), Father Lucian Petrovich (Vallaki)
- **Resources:** Consecrated ground (vampires cannot enter without invitation), healing, community trust
- **Weakness:** The Morninglord does not answer prayers in Barovia. Faith is maintained through habit and desperation, not miracles.
- **Notes:** St. Andral's Church in Vallaki is protected by the bones of St. Andral buried beneath the altar — as long as the bones are there. Someone may try to steal them.

## House Wachter (Vallaki)
- **Goal:** Lady Fiona Wachter wants to overthrow Baron Vargas Vallakovich and rule Vallaki — she believes serving Strahd openly is safer than the Baron's futile "happiness" mandate
- **Methods:** Operates a devil-worshipping cult in secret. Building political support. Waiting for an opportunity.
- **Current Status:** Tense standoff with the Baron. Lady Wachter is patient.
- **Disposition toward party:** Neutral to friendly — she will use the party if she can, and may genuinely want the Baron removed
- **Leader:** Lady Fiona Wachter
- **Known Members:** Earnest Wachter (son, erratic), Stella Wachter (daughter, mentally shattered after cult ritual), cultists throughout Vallaki
- **Resources:** Political connections, cult members, a spy network within Vallaki, a devil bound to her service
- **Weakness:** Her goal is to submit to Strahd, not defeat him. She is not an ally in the endgame.
- **Notes:** Lady Wachter is intelligent, composed, and genuinely believes she is being pragmatic. She is not evil in a mustache-twirling way — she has simply made peace with Strahd's dominance.
